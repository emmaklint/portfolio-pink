Projects

Enviromind

Visualisation of trade and emission data

Enviromind visualises the relationship between countries environmental and economic data in order to reveal international relationships and provide historical and economical context to the climate debate. I was involved in the concept, prototyping and UX in this project and I made the visual design. This project was one of three nominated projects in the category Best Research Potential at C Awards 2017.

UX design, Prototyping, Visual design, Sketch App, Atomic, D3.js, Information visualisation

Academic Overview

Redesign of an existing LMS

The academic overview at KTH Social is a page where students should get an overview of their studies and see how many credits they’ve achieved as well as finding information regarding their studies. We made a redesign of this part of KTH Social in order to better fulfil the users goals. During the user study we found that lots of students creates their own tools for keeping track of their studies. We used insights from observing their tools to and insights from prototype testing to create a better overview and experience.

User-centered design, Prototyping, Redesign, Information Visualisation, InVision, Sketch App

PapAR

Magazine with augmented reality

PapAR is an augumented reality magazine which uses several different modalities to enhance the users reading experience. The mobile application uses image recognition to display different kinds of graphics and buttons on the screen depending on the content in focus. This project used existing technology and focused heavily on exploring the user experience in augmented reality.

Augumented Reality, Unity, Vuforia, UX Design, User Evaluation
